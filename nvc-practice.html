💡 NVC Tips

🎯 Observation

Describe only what happened without judgment. Ask: "What did I see, hear, or experience?"

❤️ Feeling

Name the emotion that arose. Start with "I felt..." Use genuine emotion words, not judgments.

🎯 Need

Discover what matters to you. Every feeling points to an unmet need. What do you need?

📝 Request

Ask positively and specifically for action. Use "Would you be willing to..." not demands.
